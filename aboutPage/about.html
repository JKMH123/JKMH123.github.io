About

Filmmaker/Student

I'm an ambitious animator and filmmaker seeking to gain experience to build upon my practice in animation and cinematorgraphy as well as collaborate with other talented individuals. I currently attend Minnesota State University Moorhead where I am double majoring in Film Production & Animation with a minor in Spanish. When I’m not watching international and domestic films, boxing, listening to music, or reading a variety of novels, I continue to push my craft in cinematography, directing, and animation with each new project I challenge myself with. I’m always looking for the next great opportunity to collaborate with other talented individuals and help each other out. Serving as an inspiring role model for other young kids and people of color has always been my role ever since I joined the Scouts of America. It is something I plan to continue to do as I inspire others with my art in filmmaking and animation.
